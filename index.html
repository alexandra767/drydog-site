I.The thesis

Most sobriety apps are just a number.

DryDog gives you something to care for — a pup that grows through seven life stages as your streak grows. Slip up and they wilt. Pick yourself back up and they bounce back. The same day, no shame. They've been waiting for you.

  • ✦Seven life stages, from rescue crate to legendary.
  • ✦One tap a day. Yes or no. A pet voice cheers the yes; a fresh-start sheet meets the no.
  • ✦Pick what you're tracking — alcohol, nicotine, cannabis, sugar, or anything you name yourself.
  • ✦The whole app rewords itself around your goal.

II.The work

Every check-in, kept for you.

A memory book scrolls every check-in, freeze day, and milestone in order. Pattern insights surface your longest streak, your most common trigger, and your most vulnerable weekday — quietly, without lecturing.

  • ✦Memory book — your story, chronologically.
  • ✦Insights — longest streak, trigger frequency, weekday risk.
  • ✦Streak freeze — one free freeze a month bridges a missed check-in.
  • ✦Anniversary milestones for every meaningful day.

02 · Growth — every life stage and every milestone, kept.

III.The proof

A heatmap that looks like progress.

Every sober day fills a square. The grid grows greener week by week. No streaks, no leaderboards, no shareable shame metrics — just a quiet record of the days you showed up.

  • ✦Multi-week calendar heatmap.
  • ✦Total sober days · current streak · longest run.
  • ✦Differentiate-without-color and VoiceOver-ready.
  • ✦Edit today's check-in. Slips are reversible until midnight.

IV.On your wrist

Your streak, at a glance.

DryDog rides along on your Apple Watch and surfaces a Live Activity on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island. Tap the Lock Screen widget to check in without opening the app. Say "Log my DryDog check-in" to Siri. The fewest taps your sobriety has ever taken.

  • ✦Apple Watch companion — pet, streak, and stage gauge.
  • ✦Live Activity + Dynamic Island.
  • ✦Lock Screen tap-to-check-in.
  • ✦Siri & Shortcuts — "log my DryDog check-in."

V.Yours, only

Privacy you can hold.

No accounts. No cloud. No servers. SwiftData stores everything on your device. The optional iCloud sync uses your own iCloud — we never see it. Blur the app when you background it. Disguise the icon as Notes. Face ID locks the front door.

  • ✦Zero analytics, zero advertising, zero third-party SDKs.
  • ✦Optional alternate "Notes" Home Screen icon disguise.
  • ✦Background blur when the app isn't active.
  • ✦Face ID lock on the front door.
