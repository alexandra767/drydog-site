Frequently asked

How do I cancel my subscription?

iOS Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → DryDog Pro → Cancel. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

What if I have a slip-up?

Tap "No — I had a slip." Your pup wilts one stage but doesn't die. Honesty is the only rule — your real data is the only data that matters.

Will my data sync across my iPhone and iPad?

Not yet. V1 stores everything locally on the device. iCloud sync is on the roadmap.

Can I track more than one substance at the same time?

Pro unlocks multi-substance tracking — nicotine, cannabis, sugar, or a custom label of your own. Multi-pet is also Pro: track more than one streak at the same time and switch with a tap on the carousel.

How do I export my sobriety certificate?

Pro feature. Settings → "Generate sobriety certificate (PDF)" — this creates a printable PDF showing your pet, days clean, and sobriety date.

How do I find a recovery meeting near me?

Settings → Find help → Find a meeting. We point you at the directories that keep meeting data current: Apple Maps near-me, the Meeting Guide app, SMART Recovery, Recovery Dharma, In The Rooms, and round-the-clock online meetings. DryDog does not host meeting data and is not affiliated with any recovery program — every link opens Safari or the destination app.

How do I save the person I call when it gets hard?

Settings → Find help → My person. Enter a name, a phone number, an optional label for who they are to you, and optional notes. Once saved, Call (green) and Text (blue) buttons appear at the top of the editor and a "Call my person" button shows up next to 988 in Crisis Resources. The number is stored on the device only — it never leaves.

I'm in crisis.

Call or text 988 (US Suicide & Crisis Lifeline). Free, confidential, 24/7. International users: please contact your local crisis line.
